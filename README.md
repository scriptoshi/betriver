## Key Features

### Betting System

![Games API](https://cdn.scriptoshi.com/scriptoshi/games-api-ad.jpg)

-   **Bookmaker and/or Exchange Modes:** Admin can switch between modes or use both simultaneously to maximize your revenue stream and cater to different user preferences.

-   **Games, Odds and Results API Integration:** Say goodbye to manual data entry! Our platform automatically pulls games, odds, and results from remote data api, the platform stays updated with latest games and eliminating human error in results. This automation saves countless hours of administrative work.

-   **Real-Time WebSocket Updates:** Frontend data is updated on the fly using websockets push. No need for page refreshes or delayed information - users see odds and price changes instantly, leading to better betting decisions and increased platform activity.

-   **Advanced Trade-Out System:** Give your users more control with our sophisticated trade-out calculator. Users can lock in profits or minimize losses before events conclude, similar to professional trading platforms. This feature sets you apart from basic betting sites and attracts serious bettors.

-   **Automated Settlement Engine:** Our intelligent Scheduled-jobs-based settlement system processes results every five minutes, ensuring winners get paid promptly. This automation reduces administrative overhead and improves user satisfaction through quick payouts.

### Financial Management

![Withdraw System](https://cdn.scriptoshi.com/scriptoshi/withdraw-ad.jpg)

-   **Comprehensive Payment Solutions:** Support both traditional and modern payment methods with our flexible payment system. Accept everything from bank transfers to cryptocurrencies, ensuring you never miss out on a customer regardless of their preferred payment method.

-   **Intelligent Liability Management:** Sleep soundly knowing our advanced liability control system prevents overexposure. The platform automatically calculates and enforces betting limits based on available liquidity, protecting both your business and your users' investments.

-   **Smart Withdrawal Security:** Protect your users with our advanced withdrawal security system. Address whitelisting ensures funds can only be withdrawn to pre-approved destinations, while our dual-verification system prevents unauthorized access.

-   **Custom Commission Structure:** Maximize your revenue with flexible commission settings. Set different rates for various bet types, user levels, and markets. Our system automatically calculates and deducts commissions, ensuring you never miss out on your earnings.

### User Management & Security

![User Levels](https://cdn.scriptoshi.com/scriptoshi/user-levels-ad.jpg)

-   **Professional KYC System:** Meet regulatory requirements with our built-in KYC verification system. Easily collect and verify user documents, maintain compliance records, and manage user verification status through an intuitive admin interface.

-   **Dynamic Fee Levels:** Create up to three custom user tiers with different privileges and fee structures. Encourage user upgrades by offering better rates, higher limits, and special features at each level.

-   **Granular Control System:** Fine-tune every aspect of user permissions and limits. Set maximum deposit amounts, bet sizes, and monthly betting limits for each user tier.

### Referral System

-   **Multi-Level Marketing Ready:** Launch your own affiliate program with our powerful multi-level referral system. Configure commission rates and levels to create attractive rewards for promoters.

-   **Smart Commission Distribution:** Our automated system handles all commission calculations and distributions. Commissions can be triggered by various actions like deposits, bets, or fee payments.

-   **User-Friendly Sharing:** Boost your platform's growth with easy-to-share referral links and codes. Users can track their referrals and earnings through an intuitive dashboard.
